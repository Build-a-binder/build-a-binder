A guide to make your repository Binder-ready

Share you scientific, journalistic and educational material with the world in a reproducible and interactive way

About the workshop

You will learn how others are using Binder and related tools. At the end of the workshop you will be able to take some of your content (in a R or Jupyter notebook) and prepare it so that it can be used by others by clicking one link.



This workshop is for you if:

  • You are already familiar with Jupyter notebooks or Rmarkdown
  • You enjoy sharing new knowledge with your peers/colleagues
  • You are willing to learn how to better share your materials
  • You want to know how others are sharing their materials and want to share your own experiences
